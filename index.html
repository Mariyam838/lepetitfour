Our Story

Baking with Heart
Since 1989

What started as a grandmother's kitchen experiment has grown into the neighbourhood's most beloved bakery. Three generations of passion, patience, and really good butter.

We believe bread is more than food — it's connection, comfort, and community. Every loaf, every tart, every croissant carries that belief in every bite.

Timeline

Our Journey

1989

The Beginning

Marie Dupont opens a tiny boulangerie on Rue des Fleurs with a single wood-fired oven, two recipes, and an unshakeable belief in real bread.

1998

First Expansion

Demand grows beyond one oven. We move to a larger space and add our full pâtisserie line — tarts, croissants, and custom celebration cakes.

2005

Second Generation

Marie's daughter Claire joins after training in Paris, bringing new techniques and creative pastry innovation to the menu.

2015

Award Recognition

Named "Best Independent Bakery" by the city food guide. Our sourdough wins first place at the regional artisan bread competition.

2024

Today

500+ customers daily, city-wide delivery, and a fully equipped custom cake studio. Still using the same starter culture from 1989.
